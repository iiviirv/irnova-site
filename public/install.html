Install Nova automatically

Paste one Cloudflare token and we'll build your panel on your account — the worker and database, fully set up. Works on your phone. Your token stays in your browser.

1Have a free Cloudflare account?

If not, make one free (takes 1 min). You'll need it to hold your Nova.

Create a free account
2Get your token

Tap the button — a Cloudflare page opens, already filled in.

🔑 Get my token
  1. aOn that page, scroll all the way down → tap Continue to summary.
  2. bTap Create Token, then tap the Copy button to copy the long code.
⚠️
Copy the whole code before leaving that page — it's shown only once.
3Paste it here

Tap Paste to drop your copied token into the box.

4Build your Nova

Tap the button and wait — it sets up everything for you (about 30 seconds).

🇮🇷
In Iran: if the Cloudflare page won't open, turn on your current VPN first.
Your token is used only in your browser to talk to Cloudflare. Nothing is stored on our side.
