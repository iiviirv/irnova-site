Nova Proxy Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Summary

Nova Proxy collects no data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We have no servers that receive information about you, and we could not identify you even if we wanted to.

What the app stores

Everything the app needs lives on your device only:

  • Connection profiles and subscription links you add yourself
  • App settings (theme, language, routing preferences)
  • Locally measured statistics such as ping and connection speed

This data never leaves your device except as described below, and it is deleted when you delete the app.

Network connections the app makes

  • Your own servers: the app connects to the proxy servers described by the configurations you add. We do not operate these servers and receive nothing from them.
  • Subscription refresh: when you add a subscription link, the app fetches the node list from that URL you provided.
  • Latency tests: the built-in Radar scanner and speed test send small requests to measure ping and throughput. The results stay on your device.

What we do not do

  • We do not log, inspect, or store your browsing traffic.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, location, or usage analytics.
  • We do not share or sell any information, because we have none.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs for ads or analytics.

Permissions

  • VPN configuration: iOS asks for permission to add a VPN configuration. This is required for the app to route traffic to the servers you configure. It is used for nothing else.
  • Camera: used only to scan config QR codes, only when you open the scanner. Images are processed on the device and never stored or uploaded.

Children

Nova Proxy is not directed at children and is rated 17+ on the App Store.
